About Me

In 2016, I stumbled upon web development when I started my fashion blog built on WordPress. I discovered the allure of coding through HTML and CSS. Little did I know, this simple curiosity would shape my career.

After earning an automotive engineering degree, I immersed myself in customer experience leadership roles at Xero and Mobilize Financial Services, building people and project management skills. But deep down, I yearned for a more creative outlet so in January 2023, I embarked on a coding renaissance.

Joining the vibrant #100Devs agency, I dove into collaborative projects, bringing ideas to life, from GoalTrack Pro's football-centric content to crafting responsive websites for Strategic Edge Consultancy and building Gladiator, an agile quoting application.

Beyond coding, my true treasures are my wife and our little girl, Dara. When I'm not immersed in the digital realm, you'll find me relishing every moment of playtime with Dara, as she reminds me of the beauty beyond lines of code.

This journey of mine has been fueled by a customer-centric mindset and a thirst for creativity. I embrace challenges, learn from each endeavour, and strive to make a positive impact in the world of software engineering. The path ahead is full of endless possibilities, and I can't wait to discover what lies beyond the next line of code.
